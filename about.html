About Fred

Design is how I make sense of a confusing world. The end-product isn’t an art board or web page - it’s joy. A quiet, peaceful kind of joy that comes from knowing a tedious, confusing, or troubling thing has been made warm and easy.

Huntington Beach - 2010

I discovered design in high school through cameras, guitars, and skateboards. These instruments let me turn visions into photo books, chord progressions, and kickflips. I found joy in the creative process of bringing ideas to reality.

CAD Paddle Boat - 2014

At university, I modeled 3D engines and coded suspension systems. As my world grew bigger, creativity became all the more necessary. I wrote songs with synthesizers and breakbeat drums to tell my story. My songs yearned for adventure, meaning, and understanding. “That’s it,” I thought. That’s what I’m looking for.

Los Angeles - 2017

As a management consultant in a missile and satellite defense program, I strategized system engineering decisions to government clients and program directors. The industry was honorable but my creative itch burned inside. I enrolled in a design apprenticeship in response to the joy that once comforted me.

Handmade Bluetooth Speaker - 2018

As a designer, I just want to bring joy to your world. From color palette to information architecture, there’s a story to be told. But I don’t want my designs to just tell stories - I want them to sing songs of discovery and meaning in a world filled with befuddling options and choices.
